What is Network
Accelerator?

An "encrypted relay utility" designed to protect your internet connection and online privacy.

It creates an encrypted tunnel for your data, hiding your IP address and safeguarding your real identity from being tracked or identified. Browse the internet on public Wi-Fi hotspots safely.

How does Encrypted Relay work?

It creates a secure, encrypted connection between your device and a remote server. Your data is transmitted privately without passing through your ISP. By masking your real IP address, your online footprint becomes completely invisible, keeping your activities safe and confidential.
