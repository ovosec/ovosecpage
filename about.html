Our Company

About Us

OVOSEC is the world’s most trusted Relay provider for good reason. We are privacy experts with a track record of putting privacy first.

Mission

Our mission

We strive to make the internet better than it is today. It can be free from online threats, censorship, and surveillance, as envisioned in 2020.

Vision

Our dream

is a world where people have full control of their digital lives. That’s why we humanize digital security, making it accessible to all.

Our Story

OVOSEC was launched in 2020 in response to increasing internet censorship, content control, and intrusive surveillance. In other words, they recognized a rapidly growing demand for internet security tools that are easily accessible to everyone.

What we do

Challenging the status quo in the field of cybersecurity inspired us to develop solutions that safeguard people's digital lives. None of this would have been possible without the efforts of the team behind OVOSEC.
